Visionary Engineering Leadership at Scale

Monik Doshi is a results-driven Engineering Director with 18+ years of experience architecting enterprise-grade AI platforms, spearheading digital transformation programs, and orchestrating high-performing cross-functional engineering organizations across media, telecom, and technology sectors.

Across tenures at Cognizant, SiriusXM, and Comcast, Monik has consistently translated complex business imperatives into scalable technology ecosystems — overseeing $67M+ in combined engineering budgets, leading organizations of 50+ engineers, and delivering platforms that serve 30M+ end-users globally.

Core conviction: engineering excellence is a business multiplier. Every architectural decision, team structure, and delivery model is optimized for maximum commercial and customer impact.
